Advanced Research       Can learn useful and important info about 
                        objects, rooms and mobs. As with the research
			skill you must be in a special library room
			for the skill to work.

Duration                N/A
Use                     To help learn something about people, places 
                        and things.
Syntax                  Research (target room, object or mob)
Affect                  What advanced research tells you will depend upon 
                        what it is you are researching.
                
                        Research (room)
                        Does research on the target room, this will tell 
                        you what area you are currently in and the level 
                        of the mobs that are in this area. It will also 
                        tell you the sector type of the room, if there are 
                        any extra descriptions in the room and what they 
                        are, if there are any hidden doors in the room and 
                        where to find the keys for any locked doors 
                        within the room.
                                
                        Research (mob)
                        This will tell you the level of that mob as well as 
                        that mobs class, race and size. It will also tell 
                        you the mobs level, armour class, current hit points, 
                        max hit points, hit roll, cognition, strength,
                         constitution, willpower and any skills the mob has. 
                
                        Research (object)
                        This will tell you the value of that object and what 
                        type of object it is. It will also tell you the 
                        material that object is made out of. It will also 
                        tell you the weight of the object, any modifiers it 
                        has (e.g. damage modifiers or armour modifiers), and 
                        anything else I missed out.

                        If we have a no_research flag on rooms/objects/mobs 
                        this will only affect normal research, advanced 
                        research will tell you info about even no_research 
                        things.

Skills it counteracts           None
Skills it compliments           None
Level restrictions              None
Major element                   None
Prerequisites                   Research on 95%

Practice Cost                   Can practice to 75%, costs 1 practice 
                                point for every percent. Max % is 95.

Casting costs etc               None
Required Components             None
Starts Combat?                  No
Used During Combat?             No
