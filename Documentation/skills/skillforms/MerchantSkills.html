Hire                    Can hire bodyguards to protect the merchant.

Duration                N/A
Use                     See above
Syntax                  Hire (bodyguard)
Affect                  Bodyguards are spec swordsmen mobs and have a 
                        level equal to the merchant. A merchant can 
                        hire 1 bodyguard for every 25 levels he or she 
                        has. Merchants can group with bodyguards and 
                        order them to do some basic commands. Hiring 
                        bodyguards of course costs money, 1 gold per 
                        bodyguard per hour. Bodyguards leave if they 
                        cannot be paid.
