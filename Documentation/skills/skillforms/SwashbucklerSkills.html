Advanced Weapon Technique

Duration                Permanent.
Use                     Skilled fighters have an advantage over those 
                        not trained in the use of weaponry.

Syntax                  None
Affect                  Can perform three attacks per round. The chance 
                        of success of the third attack follows the same 
                        rules as the first attack with the exception 
                        that the boundaries for a successful hit change.

                        With a single attack you have a 25% of missing totally, 
                        a 50% of hitting their armour and 25% chance of 
                        hitting and bypassing their armour.

                        The second attack would have a 35% chance of missing, 
                        a 50% chance of hitting their armour and a 15% chance 
                        of bypassing their armour. The third attack would 
                        have a 45% chance of missing, a 45% chance of hitting 
                        their armour and a 10% chance of bypassing their armour.

Skills it counteracts           None
Skills it compliments           None
Level restrictions              None
Major element                   None
Secondary Element               None
Prerequisites                   You MUST have 95% competency in the weapon type 
                                you are wielding for the skill to take affect. 
                                If you are wielding no weapon then they need 
                                the unarmed combat skill at 95%
                                You must also have weapon technique at 95%

Practice Cost                   Can practice to 75%, costs 1 practice point 
                                for every percent. Max % is 95.

Casting costs etc               Costs you an extra 5 energy every combat round 
                                that you successfully use the skill

Required Components             None
Starts Combat?                  No
Used During Combat?             Yes

Disarm                  Cause an opponent to drop their weapon.

Duration                N/A
Use                     Gives you a large advantage in a fight, as 
                        soon as you disarm them your stance 
                        overrules theirs for a start and they are 
                        dealing less damage to you.

Syntax                  Disarm (target) 
                        Disarm (with no arguments disarms opponent)
Affect                  Disarming the target causes them to lose their 
                        weapon into their inventory. They can re-arm 
                        themselves but there is a lag associated of 1 
                        combat round above the lag for disarm so you 
                        get at least 1 free round of combat on them 
                        with them unarmed. 

Skills it counteracts           None
Skills it compliments           None
Level restrictions              None
Major element                   None
Prerequisites                   Advanced Weapon Technique at 95%
                                Using a weapon type you have 95% mastery 
                                skill with

Practice Cost                   Can practice to 75%, costs 1 practice 
                                point for every percent. Max % is 95.

Casting costs etc               None
Required Components             None
Starts Combat?                  Yes
Used During Combat?             Yes

Counter Attack          (Use your opponents strength and imbalance 
                        against them)

Duration                Permanent whilst you have selected that stance
Use                     To give you the edge and to provide variation 
                        in combat.
Syntax                  stance (stance type)
Affect                  If you are in counter attack stance and your 
                        stance beats your opponents stance every attack 
                        that you parry or shield block allows you a 
                        chance to hit back using normal combat rules.

Skills it counteracts           Fury stance and weapon stance
Skills it compliments           None
Level restrictions              None
Major element                   None
Prerequisites                   You MUST be using a weapon with a damage 
                                type that fits with a weapon type skill 
                                you have practiced to 95%, and you must 
                                be wielding a weapon (can't be unarmed).

                                You MUST also have combat basics trained to 95%.

Practice Cost                   Can practice to 75%, costs 1 practice point 
                                for every percent. Max % is 95.

Casting costs etc               None
Required Components             None
Starts Combat?                  No
Used During Combat?             Yes
