Shadow Rift

                Can create a portal to the afterlife, any entering it die 
                and become spirits with the same abilities of a spirit 
                delver spirit. That is they can talk and be heard by other 
                spirit delver spirits. Typically spirits cannot see or hear 
                other spirits, or the living. This would be different, if 
                you enter the shadow rift you are a different type of spirit 
                (or have some affect on you) and can speak with other spirits 
                that can speak. Of course you can only be heard by other 
                spirits etc that can speak.
