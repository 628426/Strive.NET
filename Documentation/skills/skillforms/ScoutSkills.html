Advanced Camouflage     Allows you to blend in well with your 
                        environment and avoid being seen.

Duration                Level hours
Use                     Hide in a room
Syntax                  Camouflage
Affect                  You can only camouflage in an outdoor room 
                        (includes, forests, hills etc). Advanced 
                        Camouflage is the advanced form of hiding 
                        outdoors, but can be searched for. If you 
			speak you are revealed, if you move you are 
			revealed. If you do perform any action 
			(unless it is stealthed somehow) you are 
			revealed.

			Advanced camouflage has an advantage over
			the normal camouflage skill because you can
			camouflage yourself without displaying a
			message to those in the room.

Skills it counteracts           None
Skills it compliments           None
Level restrictions              None
Major element                   None
Prerequisites                   None
Practice Cost                   Can practice to 75%, costs 1 practice 
                                point for every percent. Max % is 95.

Casting costs etc               50 energy
Required Components             None
Starts Combat?                  No
Used During Combat?             No

Horsemanship

Duration                Until you dismount
Use                     Allows you to mount animals that can be 
                        mounted but with greater skill then most people

Syntax                  Mount (target)
                        Dismount
Affect                  Mounting an animal allows you to move using 
                        its energy instead of your own for movement. 
                        Failing to mount or dismount an animal causes you 
                        a small amount of damage (5% of your current hps). 

                        If you are attacked or attack someone while mounted 
                        using advanced mount you do not immediately 
                        dismount, instead you can fight from horseback 
                        which gives you +2 to your dex roll and increases 
                        your strength modifier for damage by 50%.

                        Mounts mounted by the advanced mount command cannot 
                        be blocked by the bandit skill and can also jump 
                        farmers fences. 

                        Mounts are and can be attracted by various bard 
                        and druid skills as they are normal animals.

Skills it counteracts           None
Skills it compliments           Mount
Level restrictions              None
Major element                   None
Prerequisites                   Mount at 95%

Practice Cost                   Can practice to 75%, costs 1 practice point 
                                for every percent. Max % is 95.

Casting costs etc               20 energy per mount or dismount
Required Components             None
Starts Combat?                  No
Used During Combat?             Yes

Terrain Attack          (Make use of your surroundings to aid you)      

Duration                Permanent whilst you have selected that stance
Use                     To give you the edge and to provide variation 
                        in combat.
Syntax                  stance (stance type)
Affect                  If you are in terrain stance and your stance 
                        beats your opponents stance you receive a 
                        different advantage depending upon the terrain:

                        City    +4 to dexterity check to determine if 
                                attack hits or opponents attack misses.

                        Inside  Strength doubled to calculate damage.

                        Field, Road, desert     Armour cannot be 
                                                bypassed

                        Jungle, Forrest         25% chance of miss 
                                                reduced to 10%

                        Hills, Mountains        Strength tripled to 
                                                calculate damage

                        Water, Swamp    Do not suffer the penalties 
                                        that all other  people suffer 
                                        whilst fighting in water or 
                                        swampy terrain.

                        Ice     +6 to dexterity check to determine 
                                if attack hits of opponents attack misses.

Skills it counteracts           Patience stance and circle stance
Skills it compliments           None
Level restrictions              None
Major element                   None

Prerequisites                   You MUST be using a weapon with a damage 
                                type that fits with a weapon type skill 
                                you have practiced to 95%, and you must 
                                be wielding a weapon (can't be unarmed).

                                You MUST also have combat basics trained to 95%.

Practice Cost                   Can practice to 75%, costs 1 practice point for 
                                every percent. Max % is 95.

Casting costs etc               None
Required Components             None
Starts Combat?                  No
Used During Combat?             Yes
