                        for every 20 levels.

Use                     Grow stronger as you sink into a killing frenzy.
Syntax                  None
Affect                  What I suggest is that each time you kill an 
                        enemy and primal rage is successful you get an 
                        affect added to your affects list of x dam roll. 
                        X is dependant upon the level of the opponent 
                        you just killed compared against your own level 
                        rounded up. I would suggest it is only possible 
                        to have 5 primal rage affects at one time.

                        If the opponent is 20 levels or more below you, 
                        you gain no dam roll bonus or primal rage flag.
                        If the opponent is between 10 and 20 levels below 
                        you, you gain 1 dam roll bonus.
                        If the opponent between 1 and 10 levels below 
                        you, you gain 2 dam roll bonus.
                        If the opponent is your level or less then 5 
                        levels higher then you, you gain 3 dam roll bonus.      
                        If the opponent is 5 or greater levels higher 
                        then you, you gain 4 dam roll bonus.

			Whilst in such a state you can ONLY kill opponents

Fury                    (All out attack, no holds barred)

Duration                Permanent whilst you have selected that stance
Use                     To give you the edge and to provide variation 
                        in combat.
Syntax                  stance (stance type)
Affect                  If you are in fury stance and your stance beats 
                        your opponents stance your chance to bypass 
                        armour increases to:
                        75%     with your first attack, where 25% is a miss

                        50%     with your second attack if this is applicable, 
                        where 35% is a miss and 15% chance to hit their armour.

                        25%     with your third attack if this is applicable, 
                        where 45% is a miss and 30% chance to hit their armour.

                        10%     with your fourth attack if this is applicable, 
                        where 45% is a miss and 45% chance to hit their armour.

                        these modifiers modify your chances as described by 
                        the combat system and as described by the use of extra 
                        attack skills per round in combat (ie combat technique 
                        for spec soldier)

Skills it counteracts           Weapon stance and quick stance
Skills it compliments           None
Level restrictions              None
Major element                   None

Prerequisites                   You MUST be using a weapon with a damage type 
                                that fits with a weapon type skill you have 
                                practiced to 95%, and you must be wielding 
                                a weapon (can't be unarmed).

                                You MUST also have combat basics trained to 95%.

Practice Cost                   Can practice to 75%, costs 1 practice point for 
                                every percent. Max % is 95.

Casting costs etc               Double the energy spent per combat round.
Required Components             None
Starts Combat?                  No
Used During Combat?             Yes
