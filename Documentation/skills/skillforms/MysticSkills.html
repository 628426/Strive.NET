Portent                 Mystics are sometimes able to tell a little 
                        about the future from the corpses of dead 
                        animals and even humans.

Duration                N/A
Use                     By understanding the future the mystics can better 
                        prepare themselves for what is to come.

Syntax                  Cast ‘portent’ 
Affect                  For every corpse in the room when portent is cast 
                        the mystic gets a random boon:

			1) gains level / 3 hit points (rounded up). 
			2) gains level / 3 energy points (rounded up). 
			3) a negative affect is removed from the mystic
			4) double duration of a positive affect on the mystic

			The mystic is "lagged" for 1 round for every 
                        corpse in the room and the corpses are all 
                        disposed of. 

Skills it counteracts           None
Skills it compliments           None
Level restrictions              None
Major element                   None
Prerequisites                   Dispose on 100%

Practice Cost                   Can practice to 75%, costs 1 practice point 
                                per percent with a max of 95%

Casting costs etc               30 energy
Required Components             None
Starts Combat?                  No
Used During Combat?             No
