Trade Veto

                Can stop certain objects from being sold/bought in an area or 
                by certain people (like outlaws... A merchant from a city can 
                refuse a player buying in that area for a certain period)

Denounce

                Guards in a city attack or attempt to apprehend any denunciated 
                people. If they have committed no crime they would be fined
                a small amount of gold for disorderly behaviour and released
                when the fine is paid.
Back to Top Next Previous 
Tax

                Allows the master merchant to place a tax or markup on the 
                objects sold in any shop he or she owns. The tax of course 
                goes directly to them.
Back to Top Next Previous 
Customs

                Can impose a tax upon the use of the steer option on a vehicle 
                they own. The pc or mob must pay the amount of money (which 
                goes to the merchant) before they can steer the vehicle.
