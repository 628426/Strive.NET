short blade weapons     When a person with this skill is using a short 
                        bladed weapon (ie it has the damage type of 
                        stab or pierce) they gain an advantage to 
                        their chance to hit.

Duration                N/A
Use                     To allow people to train in the use of weapons 
                        and make sure that only the people who should   
                        be able to realistically use a weapon can use it.

Syntax                  None
Affect                  Whilst wielding a short bladed weapon the person 
                        gets double their existing hit roll, so twice as 
                        many tries to try and hit an opponent if the skill 
                        is used successfully.

Skills it counteracts           None
Skills it compliments           None
Level restrictions              None
Major element                   None
Prerequisites                   None

Practice Cost                   Can practice to 75%, costs 1 practice point 
                                for every percent. Max % is 95.
Casting costs etc               None
Required Components             None
Starts Combat?                  No
Used During Combat?             Yes

Syntax                  Hide

                        is the basic form of hiding and a successful 
                        search will uncover your hiding spot. If you speak

                        somehow) you are revealed.

Dirty Tactics           Rogues dont fight much, but when they do, they
                        dont fight fair.

Duration                N/A
Use                     See above
Syntax                  
Affect                  During combat, this skill has a chance of 
                        triggering and performing one of the following
                        actions:

                        Dirt Kick:      Blinds opponent for one round.
                        Trip:           Lags opponent for one round, they have
                                        to get up to continue fighting.
                        Cheap Shot:     Hit opponent in a painful position
                                        causes them to lose a round of attacks.
                        Bite:           Does some extra damage to opponent.
                        Headbutt:       Does some extra damage.
                        Tackle:         Strength check against opponent
                                        if successful, disarms opponent and
                                        causes them to lose their attacks until
                                        re-armed.

                        Dirty tactics have a 30% chance of occurring during each
                        round of combat. For a dirty tactic to occur it must
                        first make the 30% then succeed in a check against
                        their proficiency in the skill. Finally they must make
                        any checks required by the individual dirty tactic they
                        are to employ.

Skills it counteracts           None
Skills it compliments           None
Level restrictions              None
Major element                   None
Prerequisites                   None
Practice Cost                   Can practice to 75%, costs 1 practice point 
                                for every percent. Max % is 95.

Casting costs etc               None
Required Components             None
Starts Combat?                  No
Used During Combat?             No
