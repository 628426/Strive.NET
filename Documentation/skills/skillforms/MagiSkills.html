Levitate

                Ability to maneuver over various obstacles by hovering 
                into the air, certainly nothing even close to as good as 
                fly, but useful in some cases. Perhaps people with swords 
                etc can't even hit them while they hover in the air, only 
                those with missile weapons or spells could. Whilst levitating 
                in this case the magi should not be able to cast other spells, 
                having to concentrate on the levitation. Or perhaps it just 
                lets the magi lose nothing when fighting in shallow water and 
                swamp and means they can avoid ground based traps and pass 
                fences and intercept attempts.

Counterspell

                Magi can prepare a single counterspell and it will trigger 
                on the next spell cast with them as the target.. So they 
                can’t counter room or area affects with it. It will only 
                counter a single spell at a time, that is the counterspell
                is used up when a single spell is cast on you, and you
                have to recast it if you want to nullify the next spell.
