Called Shot             Skilled archers can hit very small targets 
                        from long distances.

Duration                N/A
Use                     Do more damage with a single arrow
Syntax                  shoot (direction) (distance) (target opponent) (location)
Affect                  There are four locations they are:
        
                        Head    20% chance of success, if successful 
                        you deal damage equal to targets max hps minus 
                        their armour class.

                        Legs    30% chance of success, if successful 
                        the target cannot move until fully healed and 
                        they take 25% of their max hps damage.

                        Arms    30% chance of success, if successful 
                        the target cannot wield a weapon, hold a shield 
                        or hold an object until fully healed and they take 
                        25% of their max hps damage.
        
                        Body    30% chance of success, if successful the 
                        targets worn on body armour is destroyed (not 
                        beyond repair) and they ake 50% of their max 
                        hps damage.

                        Called shots lag the archer for three combat rounds. 
                        If the called shot misses you have a chance of 
                        hitting them normally. You must of course succeed 
                        with your missile weapons and missile weapons 
                        mastery and called shot skills before the % chances 
                        are taken into account.
        
Skills it counteracts           None
Skills it compliments           Missile Weapons, Eagle eye
Level restrictions              Must be level 25
Major element                   None
Prerequisites                   You MUST have missile weapons mastery on 95% 

Practice Cost                   Can practice to 75%, costs 1 practice point 
                                for every percent. Max % is 95.

Casting costs etc               None
Required Components             Arrows to fire
Starts Combat?                  No
Used During Combat?             No
